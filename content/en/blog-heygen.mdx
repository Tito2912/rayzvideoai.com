<div class="container">
<h2 id="usages-title">Relevant Use Cases</h2>
<ul class="bullets">
<li><strong>AI "Studio" Presenter</strong> for courses, onboarding and internal announcements: a credible avatar reads a script faithful to your brand, with rhythm, pauses and eye contact.</li>
<li><strong>Multilingual Localization</strong>: adapt a FR video to EN/ES/DE while maintaining <em>lip-sync</em> and consistent voice tone, ideal for international deployments.</li>
<li><strong>Product Marketing</strong>: quickly create variations of a demo in multiple formats (16:9/9:16) with hook variations, angles and CTAs.</li>
<li><strong>Support &amp; Knowledge</strong>: reusable explanatory mini-capsules (video FAQ, "How-to") to reduce team workload.</li>
</ul>
<p>Access to key modules (Studio, <em>Streaming Avatar</em>, <em>Personalized Video</em>, <em>Video Translation</em>) is direct, which streamlines discovery and onboarding.</p>

<section aria-labelledby="forces-title" class="intro" id="forces">
<div class="container">
<h2 id="forces-title">Strengths &amp; Limitations</h2>
<h3>Strengths</h3>
<ul class="bullets">
<li><strong>Avatar Quality</strong> (recent generations) and great variety (stock + personalized avatars).</li>
<li><strong>Translation &amp; Dubbing</strong> integrated with natural rendering and lip synchronization.</li>
<li><strong>Ecosystem</strong> in progress (API, streaming avatars, frequent updates).</li>
<li><strong>Plans</strong> adapted (Free/Creator/Team/Enterprise) to test then scale according to usage.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Limitations to Know</h3>
<ul class="bullets">
<li>Like any avatar generator, a <strong>poorly paced script</strong> or <strong>bad prosody</strong> is noticeable: refine writing and punctuation.</li>
<li><strong>Image rights</strong> remain to be secured if you clone yourself or use third-party faces/voices.</li>
<li><strong>Cost</strong> increases with volume and advanced options (translation, resolutions, durations): plan a realistic monthly budget.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>
<section aria-labelledby="workflow-title" class="intro" id="workflow">
<div class="container">
<h2 id="workflow-title">Step-by-Step Workflows</h2>
<h3>1) "Presenter" Video from Script</h3>
<ol class="bullets">
<li><strong>Prepare the script</strong> (short sentences, expressive punctuation).</li>
<li><strong>Choose the avatar</strong> (library or personal avatar) and the <strong>voice</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Set the frame</strong> and import images/B-rolls if necessary.</li>
<li><strong>Generate</strong> a first version, adjust prosody and sensitive passages.</li>
<li><strong>Export</strong> in 16:9, then create 9:16 versions for Shorts/Reels.</li>
</ol>
<h3>2) Translation &amp; Lip-sync of Existing Video</h3>
<ol class="bullets">
<li>Import source video + target language.</li>
<li>Adjust <strong>speed</strong> and <strong>pronunciations</strong> (glossary).</li>
<li>Check <strong>lip sync</strong> and intonation; regenerate if needed.</li>
<li>Add subtitles and multi-language deliverables.</li>
</ol>
<h3>3) Mass Personalized Videos</h3>
<ul class="bullets">
<li>Prepare a <strong>template</strong> + a <strong>list of variables</strong> (first name, company, offer).</li>
<li>Automate via <strong>API</strong> or no-code integrations; trigger from CRM.</li>
</ul>
