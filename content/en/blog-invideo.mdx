<section aria-labelledby="usages-title" class="intro" id="usages">
<div class="container">
<h2 id="usages-title">Relevant Use Cases</h2>
<ul class="bullets">
<li><strong>UGC/Quick Ads</strong>: generate scripts and edit short formats for acquisition/retargeting.</li>
<li><strong>Seasonal Promotions</strong>: create campaign variations with multiple hooks and visual variants (A/B).</li>
<li><strong>Recurrent Social Content</strong>: thematic series (tips, news, top 3) at high frequency.</li>
<li><strong>Clips &amp; Repurposing</strong>: recycle lives, podcasts and interviews into subtitled excerpts.</li>
</ul>
<p>InVideo's strength is to <strong>quickly orchestrate</strong> typical sequences (intro/points/CTA) relying on models and an AI engine that proposes text, cuts and titles.</p>
</div>
</section>
<section aria-labelledby="forces-title" class="intro" id="forces">
<div class="container">
<h2 id="forces-title">Strengths &amp; Limitations</h2>
<h3>Strengths</h3>
<ul class="bullets">
<li><strong>Wide choice of templates</strong> for marketing/ads/social, with style variations.</li>
<li><strong>AI</strong> to generate script, voice, subtitles and assist editing.</li>
<li><strong>Learning curve</strong> short for marketing teams or solo creators.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Limitations to Know</h3>
<ul class="bullets">
<li>"Express" avatars remain basic compared to HeyGen/Akool.</li>
<li>Results may appear <strong>generic</strong> without customization (brand kit, B-rolls, typography).</li>
<li>Cost depends on <strong>credits</strong> and AI minutes; plan monthly monitoring.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>
<section aria-labelledby="workflow-title" class="intro" id="workflow">
<div class="container">
<h2 id="workflow-title">Step-by-Step Workflows</h2>
<h3>1) Short Ad Video from Prompt</h3>
<ol class="bullets">
<li>Write the <strong>brief</strong> (product, angle, benefits, proof, CTA).</li>
<li>Choose an <strong>AI workflow</strong> or template adapted to the channel.</li>
<li>Generate a first version, adjust titles, cuts and rhythm.</li>
<li>Test 2–3 hooks and 2 CTAs, export in 9:16 and 1:1.</li>
</ol>
<h3>2) Weekly Social Series</h3>
<ol class="bullets">
<li>Create a <strong>master template</strong> (intro/outro/typography/colors) + a <strong>calendar</strong>.</li>
<li>Automate imports (Drive/Cloud), generate subtitles and check readability.</li>
</ol>
