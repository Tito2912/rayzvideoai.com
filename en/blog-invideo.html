InVideo AI: Templates, Generative & Fast Editing

InVideo combines ready-to-use templates and AI functions (generative, basic avatars, voice/subtitles) to accelerate production of ads, social, promo content. This guide covers use cases, workflows, checklists, limitations, comparisons and FAQ.

Relevant Use Cases

  • UGC/Quick Ads: generate scripts and edit short formats for acquisition/retargeting.
  • Seasonal Promotions: create campaign variations with multiple hooks and visual variants (A/B).
  • Recurrent Social Content: thematic series (tips, news, top 3) at high frequency.
  • Clips & Repurposing: recycle lives, podcasts and interviews into subtitled excerpts.

InVideo's strength is to quickly orchestrate typical sequences (intro/points/CTA) relying on models and an AI engine that proposes text, cuts and titles.

Strengths & Limitations

Strengths

  • Wide choice of templates for marketing/ads/social, with style variations.
  • AI to generate script, voice, subtitles and assist editing.
  • Learning curve short for marketing teams or solo creators.

Limitations to Know

  • "Express" avatars remain basic compared to HeyGen/Akool.
  • Results may appear generic without customization (brand kit, B-rolls, typography).
  • Cost depends on credits and AI minutes; plan monthly monitoring.

Step-by-Step Workflows

1) Short Ad Video from Prompt

  1. Write the brief (product, angle, benefits, proof, CTA).
  2. Choose an AI workflow or template adapted to the channel.
  3. Generate a first version, adjust titles, cuts and rhythm.
  4. Test 2–3 hooks and 2 CTAs, export in 9:16 and 1:1.

2) Weekly Social Series

  1. Create a master template (intro/outro/typography/colors) + a calendar.
  2. Automate imports (Drive/Cloud), generate subtitles and check readability.

Templates & Libraries

InVideo highlights a large catalog of marketing templates and online editing tools to quickly adapt your content to the target platform.

  • Library of models by objectives (ads, promotions, social, e-commerce).
  • Practical functions: subtitles, voice-over, media addition, multi-format exports.
  • Possibility of generative AI (depending on plan) to accelerate ideation and editing.
